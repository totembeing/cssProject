    How we started

    Shonen Saga was born in a college dorm room when a student with a passion for manga realized how hard it was for fans to find their favorite series locally. Determined to make manga more accessible, they began curating collections and selling them online, starting with just a few volumes sourced from thrift stores and imports. With the overwhelming support of fellow students and anime club members, Shonen Saga grew into a thriving hub for manga enthusiasts everywhere.
